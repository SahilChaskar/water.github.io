Nature Awareness #lyfe.io

Want to help but are unable to donate money?

Our partnership program allows you to help our community through social activities by spreading awareness of the state of our nature.

How can you apply for a partnership program?

Through social networks, by spreading awareness through posts / stories with the tag #lyfe.io you apply for our program. On a monthly basis, we announce the winner when they expect valuable prizes.

Application conditions?

- Post with tag #lyfe.io and also tag as
- Unlocked profile (instagram)

How and when we choose winner ?

We score each post according to the following criteria:
- Instagram story 1p
- Instagram post 2p
- Twitter post 2p
At the end of each month, the 10 members with the largest number are awarded.
They are notified via email and our discord server.

What are the rewards?

Premium package on our website. Role and badge on our discord server with various additional functions for discord bots.
